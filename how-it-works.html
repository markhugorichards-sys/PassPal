Frequently asked questions

Is Passd free for learner drivers?

Yes. Passd is completely free for learner drivers to search, compare and contact driving instructors. No account or payment is ever required.

Are driving instructors on Passd DVSA registered?

Yes. Every instructor listed on Passd is verified against the DVSA ADI register before their profile is approved and published.

How does Passd rank driving instructors?

Passd ranks instructors by a combination of pass rate, response time, profile completeness and learner reviews. Premium instructors are guaranteed placement in positions 1 to 3 for their postcode area.

Can I find an automatic driving instructor on Passd?

Yes. You can filter results by manual or automatic transmission, as well as intensive courses, nervous driver support and accessibility requirements.

What is a good pass rate for a driving instructor?

The UK national average first-time pass rate is around 48%. A driving instructor with a pass rate above 60% is considered strong. Passd displays each instructor's verified pass rate so you can compare directly.

How much do driving lessons cost?

Driving lesson prices in the UK typically range from £30 to £50 per hour depending on location. London and South East instructors tend to charge more. Passd shows you each instructor's hourly rate upfront so there are no surprises.
